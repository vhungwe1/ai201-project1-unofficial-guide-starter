Professor: Paul Schmitz
Course: SES (Social, Environmental, and Sustainability)
School: Babson College

Review 1:
Great professor. Really knows a lot about his material. Can be lecture-heavy at times, but classes are always discussion-oriented. Keep up with the pre-class readings and don't be afraid to participate. He may seem intimidating, but he's actually really nice and welcoming.

Review 2:
Very fun class, just a lot of readings with pop quiz that needs to be mandatory, however love this guy!

Review 3:
Awesome professor you'll learn a lot. Lots of readings. Very understanding guy.

Review 4:
He is a great professor, very energetic and makes class interesting. However he is quite a tough grader and gives a lot of content to read! Beware of his pop quizzes, he asks detailed questions from books. No midterms, very tough final.

Review 5:
Prof. Schmitz is a very nice guy, quirky and fun. In SES his exam questions are tough. Requires advanced, synthetic based thinking. Always goes over class time. Accessible and helpful outside class!
