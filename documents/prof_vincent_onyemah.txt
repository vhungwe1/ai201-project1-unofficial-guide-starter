Professor: Vincent Onyemah
Course: Marketing/Sales
School: Babson College

Review 1:
Vini is an AMAZING, funny guy and his classes are undoubtedly engaging. However, his grading policy is VERY heavy on participation. Be prepared to speak up every single class, and bring in new ideas if you want a good participation grade. He keeps track of every class. Overall an amazing guy and very knowledgeable. You will learn a lot.

Review 2:
Best Professor there is at Babson. Extremely enthusiastic and energy fills the room. He is so passionate about what he teaches and truly cares about his students. Very knowledgeable about the subject, but also knows how to approach the topics in a strategic manner. Take him and you will get so much more than just marketing. He is truly LOVED.

Review 3:
Vini the Pooh!! An amazing mentor and friend. If you have the opportunity to join one of Vini's courses take it! Engaging discussion and great stories from Vini's personal and professional experiences. My biggest takeaway: Humans are humans everywhere. Thank you Vini.
