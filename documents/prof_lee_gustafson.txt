Professor: Lee Gustafson
Course: Marketing
School: Babson College

Review 1:
I hated him. I was very confused after attending his class. He only talks, but nothing about marketing comes out. He brings several guest speakers but doesnt really teach you anything. There is A LOT of homework and group work. I did not like him.

Review 2:
Professor Gustafson is super funny and keeps things engaging. As long as you do the pre work and participate, an A is very doable. There is extra credit and a group project in the second half of the semester. He's so funny and chill, would def take again.

Review 3:
He is genuinely so funny, kind and doesn't take himself too seriously! I really loved him. The guest speakers were really cool, and the quizzes were very fair, and he let us bring a cheat sheet. 10/10 would recommend. One of my faves at Babson so far.
