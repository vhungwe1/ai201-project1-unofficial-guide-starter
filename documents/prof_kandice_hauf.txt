Professor: Kandice Hauf
Course: FCI (Foundations of Critical Inquiry)
School: Babson College

Review 1:
Pretty easy class. She is nice but has favorites. Its hard to get above a 93 on all of the essays but its also hard to get below an 85 on them. The discussions in class are easy to wing if you didn't do the assigned readings.

Review 2:
She gives a lot of work and rarely gives above 90s on papers. She is sweet but does have favorites. Her exams are very easy but the class does require a lot of work and gives ALOT of readings and an assignment online for every class. Her pop quizzes are easy. There are easier FCI professors out there.

Review 3:
Discussions every class on the readings. Very educated, but the classes get very repetitive. If you do your homework, you'll be fine. She will call you out if you're late or if you don't have your homework completed. Lots of book reading assigned but you only need to participate once a class.
